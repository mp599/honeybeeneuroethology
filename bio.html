Principal Investigator

Marco Paoli, Ph.D

CNRS Junior Professor Chair · Centre des Sciences du Goût et de l'Alimentation (CSGA), Dijon, France

I am interested in olfactory coding, perception, and odor-guided behaviors, and in how these cognitive processes are shaped by learning, experience, and internal state. I work with the invertebrate olfactory system — honey bees, cockroaches, fruit flies — a tractable model for studying the neural representation of an olfactory stimulus and its dynamic changes. To explore the neuronal and molecular mechanisms of odor perception and learning, I combine behavioral analysis, in vivo functional calcium imaging, neuroanatomy, pharmacology, and neural network modeling.
