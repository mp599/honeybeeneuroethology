Workshop June 2026

TRex Workshop: Markerless Animal Tracking

The lab hosted a 3-day hands-on workshop on TRex, a markerless multi-animal tracking software, taught by its developers Dr. Tristan Walter and Dr. Angela Albi (Max Planck Institute for Animal Behavior, Konstanz, Germany). Held at CSGA and supported by the Forthem Alliance, the course brought together PhD students, postdocs, and researchers in biology, neuroscience, and ethology for hands-on training in state-of-the-art, tag-free tracking techniques.
